# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone participating in this project.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Communicate clearly and professionally
- Be mindful of others' time and effort

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abuse
- Personal attacks or insults
- Spam or intentionally low-quality contributions

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainers.
